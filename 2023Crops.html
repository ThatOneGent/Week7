2023 List of Crops

After much debate, planning, and taste testing... the plan going forward is to limit variety. Focusing mostly on what we truely enjoyed.

Which means...

TINY THINGS!

We loved just about everything we planted last year that was tiny in size and stature. Because all of it was BIG ON TASTE

Name Description Personal Thoughts for coming year
Great White Tomato Large Tomato that is fleshy but sweet Grow at least one every year, they can get over a lb per fruit.
Spoon Tomato A very small tomato that grows in small groupings Huge hit. Only did one plant, will likely do multiple this year.
Cucamelon Mexican sour cucumber. Looks like a tiny little watermelon. Grows on a vine. Another huge hit. Ate them off the vine whiel gardening for a quick snack.
Chocolate Habanero Higher Scoville count than regular Habanero. Smokey flavor. A must each year. My favorite type of hot pepper. Perfect in chili to give it a smokey rich heat.
White Currant Tomato Grape tomato that grow multiple on small vines. Lower in acid. Super sweet, and rich flavor. Will plant more than one plant this year.
Jelly Melon Fruit grows on vines. Turns deep orange when ripe, and has small spiny "horns" on it. Pump inside looks like Lime Jello A new addition this year. Something weird to try and give away to folks to also try.
Strawberries Strawberries are strawberries... Good success with a small basket planet in the greenhouse. Did not create large amounts of fruit, probably need more plants.

And more to come....
